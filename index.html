About Our School

Building futures through innovative education and caring community partnerships

Our Mission

Hepviwille Precious Treasure School is dedicated to providing exceptional educational experiences that inspire lifelong learning, critical thinking, and personal growth. We believe every child has unique potential waiting to be discovered and nurtured.

Our Vision

To be the leading elementary school in our community, recognized for academic excellence, innovative teaching practices, and our commitment to developing confident, compassionate, and capable citizens.

Our Values

We are committed to excellence, integrity, respect, collaboration, and innovation. These values guide everything we do, from classroom instruction to community engagement.

Academic Programs

Comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge and inspire every student

Core Curriculum

Our comprehensive academic program includes Mathematics, English Language Arts, Science, Social Studies, and Physical Education. We use research-based teaching methods and differentiated instruction to meet diverse learning needs.

Enrichment Programs

Beyond core subjects, we offer Art, Music, Library Sciences, Technology Integration, and Foreign Language exposure to broaden our students' educational experience and cultural awareness.

Assessment & Progress

Regular assessment and progress monitoring ensure that every student receives appropriate support and challenge. We maintain detailed records of student growth and communicate regularly with families.

Special Programs

Extended learning opportunities that enhance student growth and development

After-School Activities

Our after-school programs include homework assistance, sports clubs, art classes, STEM activities, and music ensembles. These programs provide safe, supervised environments for continued learning and social development.

Summer Learning

Comprehensive summer programs prevent learning loss while providing enrichment opportunities. Students can explore new subjects, strengthen skills, and enjoy educational field trips and activities.

Special Education Services

We provide individualized support for students with diverse learning needs, ensuring every child has access to appropriate educational services and accommodations for success.

Our Faculty

Dedicated educators committed to student success and professional excellence

Qualified Educators

Our teaching staff holds advanced degrees and brings years of classroom experience. Every educator participates in ongoing professional development to stay current with best practices in education.

Student-Centered Approach

Our teachers create inclusive, engaging learning environments that accommodate different learning styles and celebrate student diversity. We believe in building strong relationships with our students and their families.

Support Team

In addition to classroom teachers, our team includes counselors, librarians, reading specialists, and administrative staff who work collaboratively to support student success.

School Events

Celebrating achievements and building community through shared experiences

Annual Celebrations

Our calendar includes the Science Fair, Art Exhibition, Spring Musical, Field Day, and Graduation Ceremony. These events showcase student talents and bring our community together.

Family Engagement

Regular family nights, parent-teacher conferences, volunteer opportunities, and fundraising events strengthen the partnership between home and school.

Educational Experiences

Field trips, guest speakers, and community partnerships provide real-world learning opportunities that connect classroom instruction to life experiences.

Admissions

Join our community of learners and begin your child's educational journey

Enrollment Process

We welcome new students throughout the year. The enrollment process includes completing application forms, providing immunization records, and previous school transcripts where applicable.

School Tours

Prospective families are invited to tour our facilities, meet our staff, and observe classes in session. Tours can be scheduled by calling our main office.

Registration Information

New student registration typically begins in March for the following school year. Contact our office for specific dates, requirements, and to schedule your visit.

Resources

Supporting student learning and family engagement through comprehensive resources

Academic Resources

Our library, computer lab, and learning center provide students with access to books, digital resources, research tools, and academic support services.

Parent Portal

Online access to grades, attendance, assignments, and school communications keeps families informed and engaged in their child's educational progress.

Support Services

Counseling services, tutoring programs, and academic intervention support ensure that every student has the resources needed for success.
